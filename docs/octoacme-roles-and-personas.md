# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user interfaces and experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with product goals. They bridge user needs with technical implementation throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Establish and uphold accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG compliance)
- Conduct usability testing and incorporate user feedback
- Collaborate with Product Managers to understand business goals and user needs
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and technical alignment
- Participate in milestone planning and design reviews

### Goals
- Deliver consistent, accessible, and user-friendly interfaces
- Reduce usability-related defects and rework
- Ensure design decisions are grounded in user research

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with Product Managers and Developers
- Handoff documentation (specs, assets, annotations) for engineering
- Participation in sprint demos to verify design fidelity

### Interaction Matrix
| Collaborates With | How |
|---|---|
| Product Manager | Aligns design with business goals; reviews feature specs |
| Developers | Ensures design feasibility; provides assets and specs |
| Business Analyst | Refines requirements with UX insights |
| Project Manager | Reports design milestone status; flags design-related risks |

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business objectives and technical implementation by gathering, documenting, and translating requirements. They ensure the team builds the right thing and that stakeholders and the delivery team stay aligned.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document requirements from stakeholders
- Translate business needs into clear technical specifications
- Facilitate workshops and requirements review sessions
- Maintain a requirements traceability matrix
- Support QA by defining and validating acceptance criteria
- Assist in backlog grooming and prioritization

### Goals
- Eliminate ambiguity in requirements before development begins
- Improve alignment between stakeholder expectations and delivered features
- Reduce rework caused by misunderstood or incomplete requirements

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with Product Manager and stakeholders
- Written specifications and user stories in the backlog
- Acceptance criteria reviews with QA and Developers

### Interaction Matrix
| Collaborates With | How |
|---|---|
| Product Manager | Refines business goals into actionable requirements |
| Project Manager | Supports backlog grooming and milestone planning |
| Developers | Clarifies technical specifications; answers requirement questions |
| UX/UI Designer | Incorporates UX insights into requirements |
| Solutions Architect | Aligns requirements with technical constraints and architecture |

---

## Solutions Architect

### Role Summary
Solutions Architects design holistic technical solutions that address functional requirements, scalability, security, and integration needs. They set the technical direction and standards for the project.

### Responsibilities
- Design end-to-end technical solutions and system architectures
- Establish technical standards, patterns, and guidelines
- Guide Developers in implementing architectural decisions
- Evaluate technology choices and manage technical risk
- Ensure solutions meet scalability, security, and performance goals
- Review and approve significant technical design decisions

### Goals
- Deliver architecturally sound solutions that are maintainable and scalable
- Prevent technical debt and reduce integration risks
- Ensure the technical approach supports long-term business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Architecture decision records (ADRs)
- Technical design documents and diagrams
- Code and design reviews with Developers

### Interaction Matrix
| Collaborates With | How |
|---|---|
| Developers | Guides implementation; reviews technical designs |
| Product Manager | Aligns technical approach with product vision |
| Business Analyst | Validates technical feasibility of requirements |
| Project Manager | Identifies technical risks and dependencies |
| Support Lead | Ensures operational requirements are addressed in design |

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads oversee post-release operations, including incident response, monitoring, and knowledge handoff. They ensure the team delivers a smooth transition to operations and that users receive timely support after release.

### Responsibilities
- Own post-release monitoring, incident response, and escalation procedures
- Coordinate documentation handoff from the delivery team to operations
- Communicate operational status and incidents to stakeholders
- Maintain runbooks and support documentation
- Capture recurring issues and feed them back into the product backlog
- Define and track SLAs and support KPIs

### Goals
- Minimize mean time to recover (MTTR) for production incidents
- Ensure complete and accurate handoff documentation before release
- Reduce repeat incidents through continuous improvement

### Typical Communication
- Release readiness reviews with Project Manager and Developers
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- Regular stakeholder updates on operational health

### Interaction Matrix
| Collaborates With | How |
|---|---|
| Developers | Receives handoff documentation; escalates incidents for resolution |
| Project Manager | Aligns on release readiness; communicates post-release status |
| Product Manager | Reports recurring user issues and operational feedback |
| Solutions Architect | Escalates complex technical incidents; reviews runbooks |
